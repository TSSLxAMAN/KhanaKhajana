Our Story

Discover the passion, flavors, and traditions behind Khana Khajana

Welcome to Khana Khajana

Founded in 2015, Khana Khajana started as a small family kitchen with a big dream: to share the authentic flavors of Indian cuisine with food enthusiasts around the world.

Our name "Khana Khajana" translates to "Food Treasure" in English, reflecting our mission to preserve and share the rich culinary heritage of India through carefully crafted recipes and dining experiences.

What began as a passion project has grown into a beloved community of chefs, food writers, and culinary experts dedicated to celebrating the diversity and richness of Indian food.

Founded by a team of passionate chefs and food enthusiasts

Our Journey

The story of how Khana Khajana evolved from a simple idea to a culinary destination.

2015 - The Beginning

Khana Khajana was founded by Chef Raj Sharma, who started sharing family recipes online from his small apartment kitchen in Mumbai.

2017 - Growing Community

As our online presence grew, we expanded our team to include three more chefs and began hosting local cooking workshops.

2019 - Official Launch

We officially launched the Khana Khajana website, featuring over 200 authentic recipes and cooking videos from across India.

2022 - Expansion

We expanded our services to include meal kits, spice boxes, and online cooking classes for international audiences.

2025 - Today

Today, Khana Khajana has become a trusted culinary platform with a community of over 1 million food lovers exploring the rich flavors of Indian cuisine.
